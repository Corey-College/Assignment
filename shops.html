Trafford Centre

struggling to find clothes you like? bored of eating the same selection of food? the Trafford Centre has it all! from top of the line clothing brands such as JD and River Island to iconic Jewellers that have pieces delicately handcrafted for the ones you love.

The hustle and bustle of shopping can leave you feeling irritated and stressed which the Trafford centre has a solution for! so recline back your seat and relax as you watch a visually striking movie in the ODEON cinema, with tickets starting from only £6!

Still feeling energetic? Laser Quest is a guaranteed way of ensuring you have ended the night on a high. as you enter the otherworldly battleground, you are tasked with eliminating anyone that stands in your way with your high tech laser-infused gun! but be careful- enemies can see your glowing armour as you traverse in the dark and claim points depending on the shooters precision.

Shops For Kids

It's a week before Christmas, the tree is up and the house is ready for Santa! yet you don't feel you have enough gifts for the ones you love.. don't fret! to lighten your Christmas spirit, here are some shops suitable for your little troublemakers!

The Build-A-Bear workshop accommodates the essence of what childhood should be with its boundless options, creativity and imagination that are all needed to create the perfect bear for them! With only 3 stores located around Manchester, the trip may be time consuming but is definitely worth it once met with the magic of childrens laughter, the golden lights that make you feel warm inside and the shelves of the most huggable bears you've ever seen! The workshop has 8 stations that allow for the creation of your very own, personalised bear!

The Perfume Shop

Picking the right perfume/aftershave for you is a good way to become memorable to people around you. The Perfume Shop possess a number of iconic fragrances that emit potent, exotic aromas for hours on end. The Perfume Shop can be found in the arndale centre in Manchester which is perfect for a birthday gift. Shops too far away? they sell online too! and buying online also enables the option for customers to have engraving on their fragrance bottle.
