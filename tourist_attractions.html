Etihad Stadium

Etihad Stadium is home to Manchester City football club. The average price in 2022/2023 is £130 which may seem pricey, but will be worth every penny once you feel the exhilarating rush of pride and joy for your team! Book a tour for the stadium and explore the locker rooms where famous names such as Erling Halaand and Kevin De Bruyne get ready to enter the pitch!

Manchester Art Gallery

The Manchester Art Gallery(founded in 1823) has a beautiful collection of paintings, sculptures and pottery that were founded in Manchester and transported from areas around the world! This gallery has a free entrance fee and is located in the Manchester City Centre and is great four individuals with an artistic eye that are looking to admire others work or visit for inspiration.

Museums

The Manchester Museum harbours archaeological findings that give a deeper insight into the history of Great Britain. The museum has two floors that range from ancient Egyptian fossils to endangered amphibians that are preserved for your eyes to see! There is also a learning program that delves deeper into the history of each artifact/fossil and how they were found. The entry cost is FREE so what are you waiting for!
